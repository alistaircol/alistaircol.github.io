Year 2 (DipHE)

CS-203

Topics in Computing 2

Type: Theory and Algorithms

Weekly assignments:

  • n-cube and snake-in-the-box problem
  • Digital door locks and de Bruijn graphs
  • Robotic scheduling and alternation graphs
  • Sequences, morphisms and fractal shapes
  • Permutation patterns
  • Crucial and bicrucial words and permutations
  • Combinatorial proofs and identities; bijections
  • The sigma notation; binomial and multinomial theorems
  • Bijections between 123-(or 321-) and 132-avoiding permutations

Other assignments:

  • Introduction to AI
  • Introduction to Haskell

CS-208

Logic and Algorithms

Type: Theory and Algorithms

Assignments:

  • Logic, languages of wffs
  • Logic: inference and truth, satisfaction and truth assignment; truth, lies and damned lies
  • Lambda calculus: substitution, free variables, beta-reduction
  • Lambda calculus: higher-level views and reduction, deducing a combinator, NOR and NAND, numbers and zero
  • Algorithms and complexity
  • Tree construction and search, Balanced binary trees
  • Kruskal's algorithm - minimum spanning tree, Dijkstra's algorithm - minimum path length, Prim's algorithm - minimum weight spaning tree

CS-209

User and Data Modelling

Type: Information Systems

Data modelling assignments:

  • DB design

HCI Weekly assignments:

  • Good and bad designs for alarm clocks
  • Design a smartphone based central heating controller
  • Design a microwave for visually impaired users
  • Collect examples of interfaces that are not designed to take likely human errors into account
  • List how you think emotions would affect a user using a computer system
  • Design a digital camera
  • Ubiquitous computing solutions
  • Design storyboards for an app to assist disabled people to various locations, with some of the issues that may be faced and how your app deals with those

Main HCI assignment: The aim was to design an app for a community group I, a friend, or a family member is involved with.

First I had to gather requirements, then design the app. I designed app for college radio station. Hierarchical task analysis (HTA), State transition network (STN), Red routes.

CS-210

Computer Systems and Architecture

Type: Computer Systems

Assignments: (Screenshots)

  • Report on a comparison of the performance and features of a DEC PDP-11/10 with a typical desktop/laptop computer that someone (now) would have access to 40 years after the PDP-11, and attempt to predict the performance and features of a typical computer used in another 40 years from now.
  • Report looking at the list of benchmarks in the SPEC CPU2006 discussing whether the component suite include applications that match the ways you typically use your computer etc
  • A C program for "interpreting" ARM instructions
  • A C program for showing the pipeline values in MIPS
  • A C program that acts as a simple shell
